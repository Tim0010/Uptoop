1. **Go to:** https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image

4. **Upload logo:** `assets/images/uptop-logo.png`
5. **Center logo** on the canvas
6. **Adjust size** to fill most of the space (leave small padding)
7. **Download as PNG**
8. **Save as:** `play-store-icon-512.png`

### Method 3: Using Paint (Windows Built-in)

1. **Open** `assets/images/uptop-logo.png` in Paint
2. **Click** "Resize" button
3. **Uncheck** "Maintain aspect ratio"
4. **Select** "Pixels"
5. **Set:** Horizontal: 512, Vertical: 512
6. **Click** OK
7. **Save as:** `play-store-icon-512.png`

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### If Logo Has Transparency
Your Uptop logo might have a transparent background. For Play Store, you MUST have a solid background:

1. Open in Canva or any image editor
2. Add a white (or colored) background layer
3. Place logo on top
4. Export as PNG with no transparency

### If Logo is Too Small
If the logo appears too small in the 512x512 canvas:

1. Open in Canva
2. Create 512x512 canvas
3. Upload logo
4. Scale up to fill ~80% of canvas
5. Keep some padding around edges
6. Export

### If Logo Quality is Low
If resizing makes the logo blurry:

1. Try to find a higher resolution version of the logo
2. Or use vector version (SVG/AI) if available
3. Export at 512x512 from vector source
4. Use "Export for screens" at 2x or 3x then scale down
